# Paper Candidate Upload

This note records the repository-side convention used for clean public paper
candidate uploads.

- Build command: `bash paper/build.sh`
- Candidate filename: `Garrison_2026_Emender-<git-commit8>.pdf`
- Public web directory: `~/www/emender/`
- Stable filename `Garrison_2026_Emender.pdf` is intentionally not produced or
  overwritten by this workflow.
- arXiv, Hugging Face, and public source remotes are out of scope for this
  candidate upload.
