## Method (pinned, authoritative)

- bits/byte = nats/token × log₂(e) / `bytes_per_token`, with
  `bytes_per_token = 3.918625` → constant **0.3681635882200934**.
- Source of the constant: `scripts/estimate_tokenizer_bytes_per_token.json`
  (`bits_per_byte_per_nat_per_token`), p50k_base on The Pile at 2048 tokens,
  2000-sample sweep. **No sampled/ad-hoc multiplier is used.**
- The multiplier is unchanged from the values already in `main.typ` (those were
  already computed with 0.368163588, not the old 0.391275). **The numeric
  changes below are driven entirely by additional training (lower nats/token),
  not by a constant change.** Consequently:
  - **The ranking has FLIPPED: E88 now LEADS.** Previously GDN was the
    lowest-BPB endpoint; with further training E88 is now lowest, GDN second,
    M²RNN-CMA third.
- nats/token are the **100K-step trailing average** (`trail_100k`, the paper's
  endpoint convention), regenerated by re-running the canonical
  `paper/results/figure_2/smooth.py` against the snapshot logs.
