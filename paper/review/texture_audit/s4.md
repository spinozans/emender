## Global-voice note (whole-paper stance calibration)

The paper speaks as an insider reporting from inside its own experimental program, addressing a reader it presumes already lives in that world. Proper nouns (E88, Emender, M²RNN-CMA, "the racer", "the campaign") arrive as shared history rather than as constructions the text builds for an outsider, and result-objects are handed biographies and agency ("E88 invites…", "the racer's own training path", "production instance"). The voice is also faintly defensive: it repeatedly tells the reader how to *read* a number, protests its own honesty, and pre-empts skeptical objections inside the same sentence that makes the claim, rather than confining defense to an explicit scope/non-claim structure. Underneath this is sound, confident engineering reporting — topic sentences are clean and the technical ordering is mostly correct — so the defects are a texture film over a solid skeleton, not structural collapse.
