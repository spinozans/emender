# Texture & Flow Audit — Unit s6c

**Unit:** §6 Expressivity Results, subsection 6c — "Micro and mega: the two probes bracket the mechanism" (main.typ 1524–1544) + "QA and reasoning panel at 1.3 B: parity-rate evidence" (1546–1564).

## Global-voice note (calibration)

The paper speaks from *inside its own project*: it opens in E88's world, treats codenames (E88, the Emender, GDN, M²RNN, "stitched", "the racer", "production") as shared history, and narrates a training run as if it were a deployed, versioned, still-running system with a release cycle (v0.3) and a future. The dominant posture is defensive: nearly every result is delivered alongside an imagined skeptic ("a critic can always ask", "Honest null", "Honest mirror") so that scope and rebuttal are woven into the prose rather than stated once and left. The actual claims, when isolated, are clean and well-scoped (e.g. the matched-per-token separation sentence); the texture problem is the surrounding insider ontology and the relitigation that wraps each clean claim. My unit sits at the rhetorical climax of §6, so it concentrates both the strongest clean-claim sentence and the strongest RESULT-AS-SYSTEM imports in the section.

- **"production" / "deployed" / "released v0.3" is a section-wide pattern, not local to s6c.** The same RESULT-AS-SYSTEM frame appears upstream in this very section: the §6 heading "The same separation at the deployed 1.3 B scale" (l.1288) and "the actual released production weights" (l.1293), and originates in the intro ("the 1.273 B-class production instance", "carried down from its 1.3 B production stack" l.1143). s6c inherits and amplifies it; treat as one cross-section thread.
- **"the §6 floor argument" (l.1530) depends on the §6 opening subsection** ("Capacity is non-binding at 8 M parameters", l.1125) — a self-reference within the section; the synthesizer should decide whether intra-section §-number citations are a global convention or an inconsistency.
- **"§1's class-level claim at this training budget" (l.1562)** forward/back-references the Introduction's class-level claim and §11's prediction (l.1564); the "if it exists, will emerge … §11" arc is completed only in §11, so this unit hands an open promise to a later section.
- The micro/mega paragraph is the explicit **"culmination of the expressivity section"** (l.1543): it is the join point for every §6 sub-result above it (8 M sweep @fig_s5_bars, 1.3 B fine-tune @fig_1p3b_lengthgen, symmetric-budget run). Any §6 reorganization upstream lands here.
