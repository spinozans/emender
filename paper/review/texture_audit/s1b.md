## Global voice note (whole-paper stance calibration)

The paper speaks from *inside* its own project to a reader it assumes is already standing there: the very first sentence names "E88", "the Emender", and "production instance" as if they were shared furniture, and treats a single training run as a deployed entity with a biography ("23 stitched wall-clock days", "runs 22,200 such programs per token"). Stance toward the field is defensive-anticipatory — it argues with an absent skeptic in-line ("the evidence that the pure-recurrent slot was hard to fill") rather than stating claim, scope, and stopping. The diction oscillates between rigorous scope-marking (Lean witnesses, FLOP-class, non-claims) and an editorializing register that reassures the reader of its own virtue ("keep the reading honest", "honest null"). Net: a technically careful paper narrated as if the result were a creature with a track record, addressed to colleagues already in the room.
