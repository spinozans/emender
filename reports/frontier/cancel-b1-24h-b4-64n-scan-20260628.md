Provisional replacement choice, once the scan produces clean evidence: prefer
`BATCH_SIZE=4, DILOCO_K=20` over `K=10` for the 24h replacement unless the K20
arm fails and K10 is clearly clean. Rationale: K10 is the exact token-cadence

short-cadence regimes as quality-sensitive; K20 is the token match for the prior
B1 K80 cadence and is the less aggressive, lower-merge-frequency production
candidate. This is a conditional recommendation, not a safety clearance.
