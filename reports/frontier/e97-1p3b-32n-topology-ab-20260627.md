## A/B Decision

The A/B outcome is decisive for immediate scaleout:

- Bucketed global is viable at 32 nodes from the step-383500 E97 1.3B
  checkpoint under the recommended RCCL/alt-rendezvous setup.
- Hierarchical group-size-4 is not viable yet at 32 nodes in this launcher/code
  path; it fails before first metric or first hierarchical merge.

Recommendation for 64/128/256:

1. Unpause only a conservative 64-node bucketed-global safety gate if the
   project wants to continue scaling now.
2. Keep 128/256 paused until the 64-node bucketed-global gate completes with
   merge and checkpoint evidence.
3. Keep hierarchical scaleout paused at all larger sizes. Open a separate
   fix/smoke task for the 32-node hierarchical failure; do not use
   hierarchical topology for 64/128/256 until a new 32-node hierarchical retry
   reaches at least several K20 merges and a checkpoint.

I did not unpause any larger-scale WG tasks as part of this task.
